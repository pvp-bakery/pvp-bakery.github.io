About Us

"We strive to create a cozy neighborhood bakery for you to snuggle into and take a quick break from the hustle and bustle. At PvP, you can indulge in healthy, tasty, and innovative baked goods and breathe in all of the brilliant serendipity of life."

PvP Bakery was founded by a group of University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign students in 2017. The students were motivated and had a passion to create a bakery that could offer healthy, delicious, and authentic Asian baked goods. After many months of preparation and sampling, the students officially opened the doors of PvP Bakery on February 22, 2019.
